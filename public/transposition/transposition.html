Transposition Ciphers:

Unlike substitution ciphers which replace characters, transposition ciphers rearrange the positions of characters. The plaintext letters remain the same, only their order changes.

Rail Fence Cipher

Zig-zag pattern transposition. Write text diagonally across rails, read row by row.

Keyed Columnar Cipher

Columnar transposition with custom column order based on keyword ranking.
